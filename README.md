SmartNFT — Intelligent Hydroponic Monitoring and Control System
A soil-free automated hydroponic system built on the Nutrient Film Technique (NFT), where a thin continuous stream of nutrient-rich water flows beneath exposed plant roots. The system monitors and self-regulates the growing environment using real-time sensors and actuators — no manual intervention needed.

How It Works
In NFT hydroponics, plants are placed in a slightly tilted channel. A pump continuously flows a thin film of nutrient water along the bottom of the channel, bathing the roots and returning to the reservoir. There is no soil involved — the roots absorb nutrients directly from the flowing water.

Feedback and Control System
The system has three sensing parameters, each connected to an actuator that automatically corrects the environment.
TDS — Total Dissolved Solids
Measures the nutrient concentration in the water. When the concentration drops below the optimal range, a dosing actuator adds nutrients to restore the balance.
Water Temperature
Monitors the temperature of the water in the root zone. A cooling kit is used to bring the temperature down when it rises above the safe threshold, protecting root health and oxygen levels.
Water Level — Ultrasonic Sensor
A non-contact ultrasonic sensor measures the water level in the reservoir without touching the water. When the level drops, a pump actuator automatically refills the reservoir to keep the system running.
