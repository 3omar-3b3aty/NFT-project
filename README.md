Introduction:
SmartNFT is an automated hydroponic grow setup, based on the Nutrient Film Technique (NFT). Here the root system of plants grows in a continuous stream of nutrient-filled water flowing over a slight slope and entirely free from the soil medium.

Main body: The major breakthrough in SmartNFT technology is based on its feedback and control system. It uses three sensing parameters for monitoring and controlling the conditions around:

The first one is TDS (total dissolved solids) sensor that controls the level of minerals present in water and sends signals to the actuator that regulates the levels of nutrients.
Water temperature sensor maintains the required temperature around the roots by switching on/off a heating and cooling device.
 Ultrasonic water level sensor detects changes in the level of water present in the reservoir tank, and upon detecting low level triggers an actuator to fill up the tank.
